This is my Scratch Game

Click on Scratch Logo to go to game!

In this game that my partner and I created, the goal is to defeat the enemines and win. The user,or player, is able to choose between a male and female character. Also the player can select the difficulty level of the game. The player is able to move the character by pressing the arrow keys on the keyboard and pressing the space bar to shoot at the enemies. The player must be careful as the character does health and can lose the game if their health is brought to zero by the enemy. Once the first enemy is defeated, the player will encounter the final boss which is Kim Jung Un. If the player defeats Kim, the player wins the game.

From this project, I learned how to code sprites and backdrops. In this process, I learned some algorithms that allowed the sprites in my game to move, fire their weapon, and have health. For the backdrops, I was able to create sprites that actted like buttons so the player can press them to move to the next backdrop. Also the backdrops would change when the player clicks on the sprites (characters and selecting difficulty level) and when the enemy is defeated or if the player loses. Some abstractions that I created for the game was allowing the charcters to move when the player presses the arrow keys and coding the enemies to move and shoot at the player.
