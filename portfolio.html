This is my Malware Presentation

From this presentation, I learned that ZeroAccess is a malware that opens a backdoor to the Command and Control Server of a user's computer. ZeroAccess is disguised as fake messages, websites, or programs that is unknowingly downloaded by the user. Once the hacker has access to the compromised computer, the hacker adds the compromised computer to a wider botnet that is used by hackers to make money through pay-per-click advertising and bitcoin mining. The best advice to stay away from this is to always be careful when surfing the web, stay away from suspicious websites, and maintain an updated, quality antivirus program and your computer. For the overall malware learning process, I learned that you have to be careful what you do on the internet, looking at fake links sent to your email, updating your computer's security when given, and not downloading shading things.
