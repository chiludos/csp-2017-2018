In this game, the goal is to defeat the enemines and win. The user,or player, is able to choose between a male and female character. Also the player can select the difficulty level of the game. The player is able to move the character by pressing the arrow keys on the keyboard and pressing the space bar to shoot at the enemies. The player must be careful as the character does health and can lose the game if their health is brought to zero by the enemy. Once the first enemy is defeated, the player will encounter the fianl boss which is Kim Jung Un. If the player defeats Kim, the player wins the game.
